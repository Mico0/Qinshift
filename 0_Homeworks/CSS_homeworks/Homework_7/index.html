Meet your Critics

Sir Pawsington McWhiskers

Formerly a film historian at the "Cat Film Academy." Renowned for his love of period dramas and classic cinema. Distinguished in feline film circles, Sir Pawsington has been writing critiques for "Cat Academy Weekly" for over a decade. He specializes in analyzing storytelling and character development in classics.

Style: Analytical and verbose reviews.

Favorite Genres: Period dramas and epic tales.

Whisker "Smokey" Clawford

Self-proclaimed "Voice of the Alley." He critiques from the perspective of the everyday feline. With a sharp tongue and wit, Smokey built his reputation reviewing blockbusters on "FelineTube." He’s adored for his humorous takes and accessible critiques.

Style: Witty and humorous reviews.

Favorite Genres: Action and thrillers.

Lady Felicity Furrow

An indie film enthusiast, with expertise in noir and experimental cinema.Known for her poetic reviews, Lady Felicity earned her fame critiquing arthouse films and mysteries. Her enigmatic style keeps readers intrigued.

Style: Poetic and cryptic critiques.

Favorite Genres: Noir and experimental cinema.

About Cosmeow Space Agency

At Cosmeow Space Agency, we believe cats are natural explorers.
With their agility, curiosity, and impeccable balance, they are perfect candidates for space exploration.
Founded in the spirit of discovery, CSA is dedicated to pushing the boundaries of science and imagination.

Our core missions include:

  • Developing cat-friendly space technologies.
  • Conducting zero-gravity experiments with feline astronauts.
  • Exploring extraterrestrial terrains with robotic cat rovers.

Every mission we undertake is guided by our commitment to scientific discovery, safety, and fun.

Key Milestones:

  • 2025: First cat reaches low Earth orbit.
  • 2026: Mars rover “Purr-severance” successfully lands on Mars.
  • 2028: “Clawstronauts” test prototypes of spacesuits for longer missions.
